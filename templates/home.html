Why This Segment

Mid-market real estate often falls between lending rails. We streamline introductions and deal data so good projects find the right capital, fast.

How it works

We operate as a B2B intermediary facilitating introductions, information exchange and coordination. If required, can assist real estate developers with preparation of investor presentations, financial models and security structuring solutions. We do not provide investment advice or arrange deals, and do not hold client money. Negotiations occur directly between Developer and Investor.

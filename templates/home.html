How it works

Developers
  1. Register and complete your profile (company, track record).
  2. Upload a project synopsis and attachments.
  3. Engage with verified investors who express interest.
  4. On request: countersign a tri-party NDA (Developer–Platform–Investor) to share confidential materials and models for due diligence.
Investors
  1. Register as a professional/institutional investor and set preferences.
  2. Filter projects by IRR, risk, location, security package, sponsor equity.
  3. Like a deal? Request a tri-party NDA for confidential data access.
  4. Complete diligence and proceed to bilateral term sheet/approval with the developer.

Our role

We operate as a B2B intermediary facilitating introductions, information exchange, coordination and, if required, can assist developers with preparation of investor presentation, financial model and security structuring solutions.

We do not provide investment advice, make recommendations, arrange deals in investments, or hold client money. Transactions, terms and negotiations occur directly between developer and investor.

Eligibility & Regulatory

Access is limited to professional/institutional investors and corporate developers. The platform is for business-to-business use only and is not a public solicitation.

By using the platform you confirm you meet relevant professional criteria in your jurisdiction.

Confidential access via tri-party NDA

When an investor wishes to proceed with diligence, the platform provides a standard tri-party NDA (Developer–Platform–Investor). Once executed, the developer may share confidential files (financial model, QS reports, title/charge docs, planning packs) with role-based access.

What qualifies as “secured”?

Projects typically include collateral such as property, land, or first/second-charge security. Sponsor equity percentages and security type are visible per project.

What’s the typical duration?

Short-term funding aligned with development or bridging phases. See each project for its expected duration and exit strategy.

Who can view projects?

Registered investors can browse full details and documents. Public visitors can view high-level information on the homepage.

Data & privacy

We use role-based access and attachments are served securely. Only owners (or admins) can edit their projects.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register as a developer?

Developers with a verifiable track record in residential, commercial, or mixed-use projects.

Who can register as an investor?

Institutional investors seeking secured, short-term real estate exposure.

What qualifies as a secured project?

Projects with collateral such as property, land, or first/second-charge security.

What’s the typical duration?

3–24 months, depending on construction and exit timing.

About RE Marketplace

RE Marketplace connects real estate developers and investors for short-term, secured funding in the $3–30M segment — bridging the gap between commercial and investment banks.

Our platform was created to make mid-market real estate finance more efficient, transparent, and accessible. We focus on developers with proven track records and investors looking for direct, collateral-backed opportunities.

Whether you’re a developer seeking flexible funding or an investor seeking well-structured risk-adjusted returns, RE Marketplace brings both sides together securely and efficiently.

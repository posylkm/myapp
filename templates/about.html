About RE Marketplace

RE Marketplace connects real estate developers with professional investors in the secured short-term funding niche (USD $3–30M). Founded by former investment bankers with 50+ years of combined experience across structured funding, interest rates, credit and financial risk, we operate as a B2B intermediary — not a broker or investment adviser.

Our role is to streamline introductions, information flow, and coordination (including tri-party NDAs), so credible projects find the right capital more efficiently.
